Beginners Guide of My Singing Monsters

Start with Basics

My Singing Monsters is a musical monsters game, by Big Blue Bubble, where all the monsters sing and produce different sounds. It starts with Plant Island and players should have to buy monsters from the market at the beginning of the game. After collecting Singing Monsters, they breed these monsters with each other and make a group of monsters. If you want to go with additional features of My Singing Monsters then you have to download My Singing Monsters Mod version Unlocked all Islands

Islands of MSM

My Singing Monsters contain many Islands with different monsters. Each monster is associated with unique elements such as Water, Cold, Plant, Plasma and many other elements. Each Island has its monsters and distinctive songs. There are about 25 Islands and the names of these Islands are, Plant, Cold, Air, Earth, Fire, Haven, Light, Faerie, Psychic, Bone, Mythical, Magical, Seasonal Island and many others. You have to place your monsters on the right Island.

Feed and Level up Monsters

Make your Monsters happy and take care of your monsters' diet. Feed them when they are hungry when you keep them satisfied they produce coins. Then level up the game and unlock new items, Monsters, Islands, and decoration items. And you can get money as a reward when your monsters are happy.

Breed New Monsters

You can breed different monsters and get new cute monsters. All My Singing Monsters sing various songs. For example, the Noggine Breed with Mammott produced Drumpler, and the Potbelly breed with Kayna produced Flowah. Some of these combinations May result in Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary monsters.

Decorate Your Islands

Decorate your Monsters Island to enhance the beauty, and add some visual appeal it can also boost the happiness of your monsters. You can purchase different decoration items and put these items on your island.

Stay Tuned for Updates

The developers regularly update their players with new features and new monsters. To keep your gameplay experience fresh and exciting stay connected with updates.
